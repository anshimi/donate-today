Our Donate Plan

Donation will be used to provide food, shelter and education.

Food for a family

Donation will be used to provide food to the flood victims. The necessary food items including baby food will be packed and distributed to the victims.

Shelter for a family

Donation will be used to help victims to bulid new houses or to rapair their houses. The temporary shelter will be arranged for the victims.

Education for a child

Donation will also be used to secure the future of children. The necessary items for their eduction e.g. books, paper will be provided to victims.
